Privacy Policy for Metaborong Wallet

Last Updated: March 3, 2025

Introduction

Metaborong Wallet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our Metaborong Wallet browser extension ("Extension").

Our primary goal is to provide a secure Ethereum wallet solution while respecting your privacy. This Extension is designed with privacy and security as core principles.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

The Metaborong Wallet Extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Authentication Information: Encrypted private keys, wallet credentials, and security settings needed to authenticate transactions on the Ethereum blockchain.
  • Financial Information: Your Ethereum wallet addresses, transaction history, token balances, and other blockchain-related data necessary for the wallet to function.
  • IP Address: When you connect to Ethereum network nodes to broadcast transactions or fetch blockchain data, your IP address is processed as part of this connection.

Information NOT Collected

We do not collect or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Health information
  • Personal communications
  • Web browsing history
  • Keystroke data or general user activity
  • Website content from your browsing

How We Use Information

The information stored locally on your device is used exclusively for:

  • Enabling you to access your Ethereum wallet
  • Displaying your cryptocurrency balances and transaction history
  • Facilitating transactions on the Ethereum blockchain
  • Securing your wallet with encryption

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

When you use Metaborong Wallet to interact with the Ethereum blockchain:

  • Blockchain Networks: Transaction data is necessarily broadcast to the Ethereum blockchain, which is public by nature. This includes transaction amounts, wallet addresses, and other transaction metadata.
  • Node Providers: To fetch blockchain data, the Extension connects to Ethereum nodes. These connections only share the minimum information necessary to process your requests (such as your IP address and the specific blockchain data requested).

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your information:

  • All sensitive wallet data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption algorithms
  • Private keys are never transmitted off your device
  • The Extension employs secure coding practices to prevent common vulnerabilities

User Rights

As all personal data is stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. You can:

  • Delete the Extension at any time, which will remove all locally stored data
  • Reset your wallet within the Extension settings
  • Export your wallet data for backup purposes
  • Import wallet data from backup

Children's Privacy

The Metaborong Wallet Extension does not knowingly collect or store information from individuals under the age of 18. Cryptocurrency wallets are financial tools intended for adults.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
